Editing Help

Each task is identified by a line starting with a digit followed by a period and then text. That text is the title and any remaining text is regarded as the body.

Any text in the body of your task with a digit followed by a notion of time such as "min", "hour", "day" is parsed and tallied for the total time.

To mark a task as complete, simply add the text of completed! in the task's body.
